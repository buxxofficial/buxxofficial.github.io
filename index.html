Biography

Buxx is an emerging DJ with bags of talent and a keen ear to provide appropriate sets for any venue. With a clear passion for music, seamless mixing and thoughtful track selection Buxx will be able to transform your dancefloor and keep people coming back for more.

After attending some of the world’s biggest parties and having a genuine connection with all forms of House music, Buxx went on to complete a professional DJ course with Granular DXB. Buxx has also further pursued his passion for music through modules in Ableton, Music Composition, Creative Audio and Mixing online with the world recognised Point Blank Music School. After taking part in a range of masterclasses and completing his first track an EP is estimated to be completed by 2022.

Buxx takes inspiration from a wide variety of Artists all with their own flavours and suitability for different types of events. Examples include Black Coffee, Hot Since 82, Eelke Kleijn, Dusky, Yotto, Adriatique and The Martinez Brothers to name just a few. Paying close attention to the current trends of Electronic Music and recent hits means sets will be ever changing and relevant on any given week whilst not ignoring timeless Music from the past.
